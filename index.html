Management

The Scranton branch is overseen by regional manager Michael Scott. With over 18 years of experience as branch manager and salesman at Dunder Mifflin, Michael maintains a friendly work environment along with a supreme work ethic.

He is also winner of multiple Dundie Awards and has hosted the event for several years.

Sales

The sales team is overseen by Head Salesman Dwight Schrute. A beet farmer, bed & breakfast proprietor, aspiring freelance bodyguard, office building owner, and former assistant [to the] regional manager, Schrute leads a no-nonsense team of salesman and has been awarded Salesman of the Month 13 times.

Customer Service

Customer Service Representative Kelly Kapoor leads her one person department in delivering excellent customer care and offering incredible communication
